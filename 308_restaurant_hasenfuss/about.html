Appitease was created in 2010 for the customer who would much rather sit and relax, than having a large meal dictate their conversation.

Appitease is a bar style restaurant that serves only appetizers, with specials that change weekly.

From traditional appetizers, like onion dip to the more adventurous palates that like squid. There is something for everyone here at Appitease! Stop by and stay a while. Call in or make your reservation below.
